Residential & Commercial Driveway Sealcoating & Repair

PROTECT YOUR ASPHALT

Sealcoating protects asphalt from UV rays, water, oil spills, and other damage, extending its life and reducing repair costs. It also enhances the appearance of commercial properties with a smooth, jet-black finish, giving your home great curb appeal

BENEFITS OF RESIDENTIAL SEALCOATING

By sealing your blacktop once per year, you protect the surface from damage caused by oil, chemicals, and nature - meaning your driveway will look new for as long as you maintain it! United Driveways uses Velvetop sealcoat throughout Long Island. United Driveways can help you design a maintenance plan for your driveway, incorporating elements of seal coat, crack seal, asphalt patching, and asphalt repair. United sealcoating is a cost effective way to extend the life of your asphalt driveway or parking lot. While replacing a driveway can be expensive, an asphalt maintenance plan is relatively cheap.
